Hang up call

This sends a SIP BYE to both legs immediately. The caller will hear the line drop; any in-progress audio is cut. Recorded in the journal with your admin ID and the reason below.

Redirect call

Drops the existing outbound leg and bridges the caller to a new destination. The caller stays connected; the called party hears the line drop. Same target conventions as the order's route_kind/route_target.
